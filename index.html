Plant Care Tips

How Not to Kill a Monstera

Monsteras love bright, indirect sunlight. Water only when the top inch of soil feels dry.

Watering Basics

Overwatering is the most common mistake. Always check the soil before watering your plant.

Choosing the Right Spot

Different plants need different amounts of light. Read plant labels carefully before placing them in your home.
